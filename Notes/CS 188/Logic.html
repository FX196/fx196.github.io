Logic

  • Entailment \(A \vDash B\)

    • In all the models that \(A\) is true, \(B\) must also be true
    • \(F \vDash T\) is true
    • \(A\) has a smaller "truth space"
    • Examples
      \(\alpha \wedge \beta \vDash \alpha\)
      \(\alpha \vDash \alpha \vee \beta\)
      \(\alpha \vDash (\beta \vee \gamma) \Longrightarrow (\alpha \vDash \beta \vee \alpha \vDash \gamma)\) is not true;
      Counterexample: \(\beta = \alpha, \gamma = \neg\alpha\)
